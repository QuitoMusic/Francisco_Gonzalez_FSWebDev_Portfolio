About Me:

Hello and welcome! My name is Francisco González, and I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer. Many years ago, I made the decision to become a Music teacher on the beautiful island of Puerto Rico. This path brought me countless exciting and emotionally fulfilling opportunities that have had a lasting impact on hundreds of Puerto Rican children, helping them develop valuable skills for their professional journeys. Unfortunately, my time on the island was cut short when Hurricane María struck in 2017. The devastation caused by the hurricane made it incredibly challenging for the community to rebuild and sustain itself for months. Despite the difficulties, my family and I saw a new opportunity and decided to embark on a new chapter in the vibrant state of Florida. Now, as a Full-Stack Web Developer, I bring to you a world of possibilities in designing and creating your webpage according to your desires. I am thrilled to offer my expertise and help you establish a strong online presence. Together, we can bring your vision to life and create a captivating digital experience for your audience. Thank you for considering my services, and I look forward to collaborating with you!
