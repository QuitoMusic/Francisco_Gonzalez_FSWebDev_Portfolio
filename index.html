About Me:

Hello and welcome! My name is Francisco González and I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer. Going back many years, I decided to become a Music teacher in the island of Puerto Rico. This decision brought me a lot of exciting and emotional opportunities that, up to this day, have impacted hundreds of Puerto Rican children into their professional decision-making skills. I wish I could have stayed longer on the island, but unfortunately, Hurricane María struck in 2017. This was a disaster for all inhabitants of the Caribbean since it was next to impossible for months to be sustainable. Luckily, an opportunity arose, and my family and I decided to move to the beautiful state of Florida. Now, I come to you as an Uber driver to follow my journey into becoming a Full-Stack Web Developer.
